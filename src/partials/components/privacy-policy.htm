Privacy & Policy

Hi, welcome to our privacy policy which applies to our customers. This policy sets out how if you are a StaticMania.com user or visitor to our site, we treat your personal information.

Last updated on March 01, 2020.

1. The type of personal information we collect

We are gathering some personal information about our site visitors and users.

The most common types of information we obtain include things such as usernames, member names, email addresses, and other contact details, help requests, comments on products (if applicable).

2. Cookies and web analytics

We use cookies to recognize and monitor users, their on-site behavior, and their preferences for accessing their website. These cookies include the IP and time of visits by visitors. Visitors to StaticMania that do not want cookies put on their browsers should configure their browsers to reject cookies before using the StaticMania website.

3. When do we need to update this policy?

We will need to update this policy from time to time to ensure that it remains up-to-date with the latest legal requirements and any improvements to our privacy management practices.

When we change the policy, we will make sure that we inform you, if any, of such changes. A copy of this policy’s latest version will always be available at this page.

4. Marketing Choices regarding your personal data

Where we have your permission to do so (e.g. if you subscribed to one of our email lists or indicated that you are interested in receiving deals or information from us), we will send you email marketing messages about products and services which we feel may be of interest to you. You can ‘opt-out’ of such communications if you would prefer not to receive them in the future by using the “unsubscribe” facility provided in the communication itself.
